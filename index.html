About us

Founded by Ishtiaque Akbar and Izhaan Salam, STEM Sphere aims to make finding the right tutor easy. We noticed the struggle of endless lists of tutors with similar qualifications, but no real insight into their teaching style.

On our platform, every tutor has videos explaining concepts, allowing you to see if their teaching approach works for you before committing. Simply browse tutor profiles, watch their videos, and choose the one that fits your learning needs.
