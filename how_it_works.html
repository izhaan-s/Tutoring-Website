To solve this issue, on our platform every single tutor on our platform have videos of them explaining concepts/questions in the subject they are teaching, this way students can see if the tutor explains in a way that they understand and benefit from the most.

All you have to do is select on our tutors profiles, preview their videos to see if you like their style of teaching and then fill out a form showing interest in being tought and the times which work best between you and the tutor and it's as easy as that!

Our approach not only saves money as you wont have to pay only to find out in the first session that the tutor you paid for does not teach in a way you understand the most.
